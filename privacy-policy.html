What Information We Collect

We collect no personal information whatsoever. VerseFlow is an offline-first Chrome extension. There is no VerseFlow server, no database, and no account system.

The extension stores the following data locally on your device using Chrome's built-in chrome.storage.sync API:

Data Why it's stored Where
Theme preference Remember your chosen verse theme (Comfort, Wisdom, etc.) Local only
Translation preference Remember NIV / ESV / KJV selection Local only
Dark / light mode Remember your display preference Local only
Streak counter Track your consecutive daily Scripture habit Local only
Saved ("This hit me") verses Keep your favourite verses accessible Local only
Journal entries (Pro) Save your reflections on each verse Local only
Pro status flag Remember whether you've upgraded to Pro Local only
Scripture Circle ID (Pro) Your shareable group ID for Scripture Circle Local only
Payment (Stripe) Processed entirely by Stripe ‚Äî we never receive card details Stripe only

‚úÖ None of this data ever leaves your device. Chrome's storage.sync API syncs data across your own Chrome profile ‚Äî it does not send data to VerseFlow's servers because VerseFlow has no servers.

Permissions We Use & Why

Chrome extensions must declare all permissions upfront. Here's exactly why VerseFlow needs each one:

storage ‚Äî Saves your preferences, saved verses, journal entries, and streak count locally on your device. Nothing is uploaded.

alarms ‚Äî Sets a daily timer to refresh the anchor verse. Used entirely within your browser with no external communication.

Payments & Stripe

VerseFlow Pro is a one-time $19.99 lifetime purchase processed by Stripe, a trusted payment processor used by millions of businesses worldwide.

When you click "Go Pro," you are redirected to Stripe's secure checkout page at buy.stripe.com. All payment processing, card handling, and receipt emails are managed entirely by Stripe.

üí≥ VerseFlow never sees, stores, or has access to your payment card details, billing address, or any financial information.

Stripe's privacy policy is available at stripe.com/privacy.

After payment, Pro status is activated manually in the extension by clicking "Already paid? Click here to activate." This confirmation is stored as a simple flag in your local Chrome storage only.

Third-Party Services

VerseFlow uses zero third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. There are no SDKs from Google Analytics, Facebook, Mixpanel, or any similar service embedded in VerseFlow.

The only external service VerseFlow interacts with is Stripe ‚Äî and only when you choose to upgrade, by navigating to Stripe's own website.

All 100+ Bible verses are bundled directly inside the extension. VerseFlow makes no external API calls to fetch verse data, meaning it works fully offline.

Children's Privacy

VerseFlow does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. Since we collect no personal data at all, VerseFlow is safe for all ages.

6

Your Data & Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control over it at all times.

To delete all VerseFlow data:

1. Go to chrome://extensions/

2. Find VerseFlow and click Remove

3. All local data is permanently deleted with the extension

Alternatively, use the "Clear all saved verses" button in the Saved tab of the popup to delete your favourites, or simply delete journal entries directly in the extension.
